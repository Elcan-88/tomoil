INDUSTRY SERVICES

As a manufacturer Tomoil understands the importance of the efficiency factor in moving machinery systems. The company knows that lubricants must be fine-tuned to fit the specific needs of a specific aggregate. Tomoil and its contractors put together a team of experts who engage in site visits and systems analyses to formulate and deliver lubricants in an accurate manner. For confident use by customers, we organise visits to the facility to ensure the lubricants and maintenance fluids are applied in the correctly, and that the materials are performing as designed. This way the company aims to protect your machinery and extend its effective period of operation.
